Why Join a Connect Group?

Community

By joining a Connect Group, you become part of a supportive and close-knit small group within the church family, just as we see in Acts 2:44-47. Together, we grow, share, and celebrate life, building meaningful relationships that strengthen our faith.

Devotion

A Connect Group helps you cultivate a deeper relationship with God beyond Sunday Services (Acts 2:42-43). It’s a space where you can dive deeper into Scripture, share your spiritual journey, and encourage one another in devotion to God.

Compassion

In our Connect Groups, we create an environment where compassion and hospitality are key. We embrace the spirit of Luke 10:25-37, reaching out to those in need, offering support, and inviting others into the love of Christ, especially those who are unchurched or seeking.

Leadership

Joining a Connect Group is more than just fellowship—it’s about growing into leadership. As you participate, you’ll be equipped to lead and disciple others, following the biblical example of raising leaders through intentional community (2 Timothy 2:2, Exodus 18:17-23, 1 Timothy 4:13-16).

What Happens in a Connect Group?

Bible Study

Discover and grow in God’s Word together.

Prayer

Pray for one another and grow spiritually.

Fellowship

Build lasting friendships over shared interests.

Service

Engage in community outreach and service projects.
